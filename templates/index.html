Welcome to TomatoCare!

About

"TomatoCare" is a supply and demand forecasting support tool for tomatoes in Nueva Vizcaya in support with Price monitoring, Agricultural News from the Department of Agriculture. TomatoCare aims to provide a platform that will aid the farmers of their decision making skills in cultivation.

What-if Analysis

What-if Analysis is the main feature of the TomatoCare web application, where users can make forecasts about future supply and demand of tomatoes.
